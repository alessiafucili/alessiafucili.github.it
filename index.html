Achiro Display

Type Design

2024

Timeless Forms of Culture.

This font combines the historical and artistic richness of Byzantine mosaics with modern typography. Inspired by inscriptions in Byzantine churches and basilicas, it brings the elegance and majesty of these ancient works into a digital, user-friendly form.

Byzantine mosaics, which flourished between the 4th and 15th centuries, are a unique art form that reflects both the artistic and the cultural-religious evolution of the Byzantine Empire.

Inscriptions on Byzantine mosaics were central to the religious and cultural life of the time. They enriched the images with meaning and provide insight into the historical and spiritual context of their creation.

Fuoriluogo

Editorial Design

2025

Memory, Identity, and Belonging.

The project explores the Giuliano-Dalmata district of Laurentina, a symbol of the memory of the Giuliano-Dalmatian exiles, with particular attention to the Historical Museum Archive of Fiume, which preserves objects and testimonies of the community artifacts capable of telling stories of identity, loss, and belonging.

The investigation focuses on an anthropological reflection on the identity dichotomy “Croatian or Italian,” exploring the social pressure that imposed such a binary choice on the locals, generating a cultural conflict with their very existence. In this context, the need emerges to transmit the testimony of forced migration and the subsequent act of reconstruction, marked by the passage of time.

A resonant relationship between image and word. On one side appears a figurative sign, while on the opposite side lies its evocative meaning, expressed in textual form. The image represents the object in its materiality and visual presence, while the text conveys its immaterial, symbolic, or emotional dimension.

Wake Up

Web Design

2025

Are you sure you know the truth?

We live in an era where information travels quickly, but not always in the right direction. Conspiracism, often associated with a certain kind of paranoia, represents a psychological response to situations of uncertainty and crisis, acting as a “mental anesthetic” that offers simplified and comforting explanations for complex and unsettling events.

Wake Up is an immersive simulation of a conspiracy website, designed to credibly but deliberately exaggeratedly replicate the tones, language, and “evidence” typical of alternative theories, particularly those linked to the moon landing and flat-Earth beliefs.

Through a sensationalist style and absurd claims, the project exposes the hypocritical rhetoric of conspiracism and highlights how easily such narratives, when cleverly packaged, can appear plausible to those navigating the web.

Paradox

3D Animation

2024

An utopia in contradiction.

The contrast between utopia and distopya is represented through elements expressed on both material and conceptual levels. The concept aims to stress the contradictions between two opposite views of the world, using the sphere as a symbol of continuity and transformation.

The effect deployed in editing emphasizes the contrast between utopia and dystopia, thus making the transitions between these two dimensions even more striking. The introduction of visual disruptions and glitches not only creates discontinuity, but also a mild discomfort in the viewer, conveying the effect of fragmented or tampered footage.
